About Me

A passionate developer, I'm curious and enthusiastic about discovering new ways of practicing my profession. Very attracted by the system and low-level aspects of video game development, I'm keen to be involved in ambitious productions.

Developing my own game engine, I'm looking to learn first-hand about the entire game development pipeline, from simple vertices to scene illumination and optimization, I'm interested in anything engine-related that could improve the experience of developers and players.
