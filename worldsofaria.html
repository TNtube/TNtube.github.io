Worlds of Aria

A multiplayer, table top RPG.

Worlds of Aria, a game by Ludogram, is a multiplayer, multiple-choice tabletop RPG where each player's choice determines the outcome of the story.
As part of my work-study program, I was able to take part in the game's development from start to finish. I was able to take part in many aspects of development, but particularly in the more system-oriented parts of the game, such as networking, SDK integration and porting the game to the Nintendo Switch console.
